# Wish List

The Wish List is where you track locomotives you want to spot. Add any loco you're hunting for, and Headcode will celebrate when you finally log it.

<!-- Screenshot: The Wish List tab showing pending wishes -->

## Accessing the Wish List

In the **My Spots** screen, tap the **Wish List** tab (alongside the My Spots tab) to switch to your wish list.

## Adding to Your Wish List

You add a locomotive to your Wish List through the **Detailed Log** form when logging a spot:

1. Open the **Detailed Log** for a new or existing spot
2. Enable the **Mark as Desired** toggle
3. The form switches to Wish List entry mode — enter the locomotive number and any other known details
4. Tap **Save**

The locomotive will appear in your Wish List until you spot it.

## When You Spot a Wished Locomotive

When you log a spot for a locomotive that's on your Wish List, Headcode detects the match and shows a **Locomotive Found** celebration overlay. The loco is automatically moved from your Wish List to your spots.

## Removing from Your Wish List

To remove a locomotive from your Wish List without spotting it:

1. Go to the **Wish List** tab
2. **Swipe left** on the entry
3. Tap **Delete**

## Filtering

The Wish List supports the same filtering as your spots list. Use the filter panel to search by locomotive number, class, or other fields.

## Export

You can export your active Wish List as a CSV file from **Settings > Import / Export > Export Wishlist as CSV**. See [Export Wishlist as CSV](../settings/import-export/export-wishlist) for details.
