# Export Wishlist as CSV

Export your active Wish List as a CSV file. This is separate from the spot data export and only includes locomotives you're currently hunting for.

<!-- Screenshot: The Import/Export screen showing the Export wishlist as CSV option -->

## What's Included

The wishlist CSV includes your **active wishes only** — locomotives that are on your list but haven't been spotted yet. Completed wishes (locomotives you've since spotted) are not included.

Each row contains:

- Locomotive number
- Locomotive name
- Power type (if recorded)
- Build year (if recorded)
- Date added to the Wish List

## How to Export

1. Go to **Settings > Import / Export**
2. Tap **Export wishlist as CSV**
3. Choose where to save or share the file

## Using Your Wishlist CSV

Once exported, you can:

- Open it in a spreadsheet app to view your wanted locomotives
- Share your wish list with other spotters
- Keep a record of what you were looking for at a given time

::: info
This export only covers your current active Wish List. For a full backup including wish list data and all spots, use [Full Backup](export-full) instead.
:::
