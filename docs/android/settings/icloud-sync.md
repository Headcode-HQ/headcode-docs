# iCloud Sync

iCloud Sync is not available on Android.

Headcode's iCloud integration is an iOS-only feature and requires Apple iCloud services.

## Android Alternatives

On Android, you can still protect and transfer your data using:

- [Full Backup](/android/settings/import-export/export-full)
- [Export as CSV](/android/settings/import-export/export-as-csv)
- [Export Photos](/android/settings/import-export/export-photos)
